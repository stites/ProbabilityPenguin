About Me

Greetings reader!

Thanks for taking a personal interest in me. To reiterate for clarity, in case you've wound up on the wrong page, my name is Sam Stites - I'm a fullstack engineer using Angular.js and an large interest in Node.js technologies. My background is in Data Science and I spend my spare time thinking about data infrastructure and how the web works. I love everything Javascript, which is something you get from spending something like 100 hours a week at Hack Reactor, as well as all things python - hailing from my work at TurboSquid.

The startup scene is one of my favorite environments. I owe a lot to Venture for America for setting me on that track, and I am a strong believer their mission.

In my spare time I love going to see improv comedy. I am a huge fan of The New Movement Theater down in New Orleans. They're some of the funniest kids on the block and I had the pleasure of learning improv from them for a year. You can also find me in a park juggling 5..6..7(!) balls while on a slackline, while unicycling, while my hat is on fire. I am a religous attendee of The Barefoot Monkey's, my old troupe, Monkey See Monkey Do convention. It really is one of the most beautiful slices of life.

Feel free to contact me at sam@stites.io, follow me on twitter @samstites, or catch me on github at github.com/stites.
